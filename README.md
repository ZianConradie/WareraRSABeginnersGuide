## Key Beginner Terms
* **BTC:** In-game currency of WarEra.
* **Work (Lightning bolt symbol):** Allows you to earn a salary by working for another company.
* **Self-work (Lightbulb symbol):** Work for a company you own.
* **Production Point:** Earned from work done in factories (by you or your employees), used to produce goods.

## 1. Basic UI Breakdown
* **Natural Regeneration Timer:** The bottom-left timer shows how long until your natural regeneration tick happens.
* **Stat Regeneration:** Each bar has numbers with upward arrows (↑) showing how much that stat regenerates every hour (10% of total capacity regenerates per hour).
* **Segmented Intervals:** You can only interact with your bars at specific segmented intervals:
    * **Hunger:** Increments of 1
    * **Energy / Intelligence:** Increments of 10
    * **Health:** Max is increments of 10 (can be less due to armor limitations).

* **Map Options:** Clicking "Options" (highlighted in green on the UI) allows for different map modes to be toggled:
    * Political
    * Development
    * Strategic Resources
    * Globe (experimental)
    * Graticule
    * Battles on map
    * Battles on layout
    * Contextual borders
    * Flags
    * Sound effects

## 2. Early Progression

### Missions Tab
The missions tab (highlighted in pink on the middle left of the UI) allows you to complete objectives to earn XP, which is how you level up. Leveling is **VERY important** for raising your damage and money earned. Try to complete every mission across its three sub-tabs:

#### 1. Daily Missions
* 10 Missions that reset daily.
* You earn **10 XP** per completed mission (the lowest of all tabs).
* A bonus reward is given for completing 7 out of 10 daily missions, consisting of:
    * 100 XP
    * 10 BTC
    * 1 Lootbox

#### 2. Weekly Missions
* 10 Missions that reset weekly (on Monday).
* You earn **30 XP** per completed mission.
* A bonus reward is given for completing 7 out of 10 weekly missions, consisting of:
    * 300 XP
    * 30 BTC
    * 3 Lootboxes

#### 3. Starting Missions
* 14 unique missions that **Do Not reset** and can only be claimed once.
* Try to complete these as fast as possible.
* Reward for completing all 14 missions consists of:
    * 500 XP
    * 50 BTC
    * 5 Lootboxes

### Daily Gift
* Every day, you receive a free gift in the store consisting of **1 Lootbox**. Make sure to claim this!
* It is worth roughly **3.4 BTC** on the market.
* **Tip:** It is highly recommended to sell all lootboxes early on so you can reinvest the BTC into your starting economy.

### Fast Leveling at Low Levels
Levels 1–10 pass very quickly because XP requirements are low. During this phase, your goal is to build your economy, **not your combat power**. 

When you gain levels, you earn skill points. Early on, prioritize placing them into:
1. **Production:** Increases what your companies produce.
2. **Energy:** Gives you more usable actions.
3. **Entrepreneurship:** Increases the number of active companies you can hold.

> **Your Early Game Priority:** Money. The first few levels feel slow financially, but this is where you lay the foundation. Ignore advanced combat for now—focus entirely on creating a stable income. With money, everything else (gear, crafting, scaling companies) becomes much easier.

## Quick Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
* Join Discord immediately.
* Focus on Energy + Production skills first.
* Check into the game at least once every 10 hours.
* Hit your daily XP limit for bonus rewards.
* Make multiple hits in a battle of your choice (or gamble and hit up to 40-50% loot chance across multiple battles).

### DON'T:
* Put early skill points into Attack or Health.
* Let your resource bars sit full for hours.
* Miss daily XP bonuses.
* Try to dominate battles early on.
* Play solo without the community.

## 3. Economics & Resources

### Starting Money
You begin the game with **50 BTC**. You can earn more by:
* Working jobs for other players.
* Producing and selling goods on the market.
* **Referral Bonus:** Before reaching level 3, you can enter a referral code for a free **+10 BTC**. *(Click your Icon → Profile → Right Arrow → Referrals. Use `StormHazard` if you appreciate this guide!)*
* Publishing articles for tips (not recommended for beginners).

### Player-Driven Market
All items (weapons, food, materials, ammo) are traded entirely between players. Prices shift constantly based on supply and demand.
* **Trading Tip:** When selling items, list your quantities at a slightly lower price than the active competition so that your offers sell quickly and top the market page.

### Resources
* **Resource Deposits (RD):** * Provides raw materials such as lead, iron, grain, limestone, etc.
    * Deposits spawn randomly and reset daily across the map, meaning they can be depleted before the day ends.
    * Factories benefit from a **+30% production bonus** if built on a matching resource region. 
    * Due to randomness and depletion, RDs are unreliable for stable production; moving your company around to hunt these down is not recommended.
* **Strategic Resources (SR):**
    * Countries can choose one resource/item to specialize in. 
    * Provides a permanent, permanent nationwide production bonus to companies established in that country producing that specific resource.
    * Bonuses grant **+3% per unique SR** and **+1% for duplicates**, stacking directly onto company output.

### Economic Skills
* **Entrepreneurship:** Used to Self-Work in your own companies.
* **Energy:** Used to Work for a salary.
* **Production:** Determines the Production Points you generate each time you work.
* **Companies Limit:** Controls the maximum amount of companies you can own.
* **Management:** Determines the amount of workers you can manage and your daily hire limit.

### How Production Points Work
* Each time you work at a company (your own or someone else's), it consumes **10 energy/entrepreneurship**.
* This activity generates production points for the company.
* As you scale your **Production Skill**, the production points gained per 10 energy increase.
* **Production Formula:** `Output = Production Skills (RNG +-2) * Production Bonuses`
* Production bonuses are amplified by Country Production Bonuses and Resource Bonuses (established regions for raw materials).

### Company Upgrades & Costs
Companies are built using concrete. Costs scale upward with every company you create ($50

* **Automated Engine:** Generates passive production points per day (e.g., Level 4 generates 96/day). **Focus on Automation early game!**
* **Storage:** Extends the maximum storage capacity of your company (e.g., Level 1 gives 200 capacity).

### Taxes & Early Setup Strategy
When employees work for a company, they pay a percentage tax to the host country, which is used for wars or regional infrastructure upgrades.

**Recommended Beginner Setup:**
* Establish your companies in the country with the highest production bonus.
* Hire **no employees** (run solo early on).
* Focus on producing **Iron & Steel** or **Limestone & Concrete**.
* Allocate your skill points strictly into **Entrepreneurship, Energy, Production, and Company Limit**.

## 5. Combat Guide

### Equipment Tiers
There are 6 gear tiers: **Common $
ightarrow$ Uncommon (Green) $
ightarrow$ Rare $
ightarrow$ Epic $
ightarrow$ Legendary $
ightarrow$ Mythic**.
* *New Player Advice:* Stick strictly to green-tier equipment or below. Higher tiers are too expensive and wasted early on.

### Equipment Types & Bonuses
*Armor durability is reduced by 1% for every hit. When it completely breaks, you receive scrap metals.*
* **Gloves:** Boosts **Precision** (Crucial to prevent missing attacks).
* **Boots:** Boosts **Dodge Chance** (Allows you to hit without using health or damaging your armor).
* **Pants & Armor:** Grants incoming damage reduction (reduces the health cost per hit from 10 down to 9, 8, 7, etc.).
* **Helmet:** Increases Critical Damage.
* **Weapons:** Modifies Base Damage and Critical Percentage.

### Precision Matters
* Every time you hit, there is a chance to miss. Your precision percentage decreases this miss chance.
* Missing a shot drastically reduces your damage output and prevents you from landing critical hits. **Prioritize precision early on!**

### Simplified Combat Formula
`Damage = (Attack + Weapon) × RNG × Ammo × Buffs × Terrain Bonuses`

## 6. Consumables

### Food (Heals HP)
* **Bread:** +10% of max HP
* **Steak:** +15% of max HP
* **Cooked Fish:** +20% of max HP

### Ammo (Damage Boosts)
* **Light:** +10% damage
* **Medium:** +20% damage
* **Heavy:** +40% damage

### Pills
* Grants **+80% damage for 8 hours**, immediately followed by a debuff of **-80% damage for 16 hours**.
* Very expensive—**avoid completely in the early game**, but essential for mid-to-late game coordination.

## 7. How Battles Work

### Declaration of War
* Only government congresses have the authority to start a war.
* Battles are fought directly on a **region vs. region** basis over a best-of-3 rounds structure.

### Ground Points (GP) System
* A round concludes immediately when one side reaches **300 ground points**.
* Ground points are awarded every 2 minutes (referred to as a "tick").
* The side holding the higher total damage output during that specific 2-minute tick secures the GP.
* The GP awarded per tick scales up as the round elongates:
    * **0–100 total round GPs:** 1 GP per Tick
    * **100–200 total round GPs:** 2 GP per Tick
    * **200–300 total round GPs:** 3 GP per Tick

### Battle Damage Modifiers
* **Bunker:** Up to +25% damage
* **National Orders:** Up to +15% damage
* **MU Orders:** Up to +15% damage
* **Resistance:** Up to +40% damage
* **Sworn Enemies:** +10% damage
* **Alliance:** +10% damage
* **Disconnected Regions:** -25% damage penalty

## 8. Military Units (MUs)
* South Africa operates 6 official Military Units, all sharing the name **SATURN**.
* All citizens are highly expected to join one early on.
* **Benefits:** MU Commanders issue live combat orders that grant bonus damage, and you can request team healing of up to **10 HP**.

## 9. Crafting System
Crafting enables players to construct new armor and weaponry.

### Materials Required
* **Scrap:** Obtained by dismantling existing gear (Inventory → Select Item → Dismantle) or via the market.
* **Steel:** Produced from raw iron.

### Crafting Customization Options
* **Random Piece:** Crafts a random equipment type from a chosen tier (cheaper).
* **Specific Piece:** Crafts a chosen specific equipment slot, but costs **double the steel**.

## 10. Tips for New Players
* Prioritize working and establishing your economy over fighting in wars early on.
* Avoid purchasing high-tier gear until your base stats allow you to deal meaningful impact.
* Join an MU as soon as possible for free damage boosts and team healing support.
* **Specialize:** Pick one definitive lane early—either production, combat, or market trading.

## Combat Stat Progression Guide

### Key Stats to Focus On
* **Attack:** Outlines your base flat damage.
* **Precision:** Increases clean hit probability (the absolute most vital early stat).
* **Crit Chance:** The frequency of critical hits.
* **Crit Damage:** The multiplier strength behind critical hits.
* **Armor & Dodge:** Lowers incoming health damage and avoids hits entirely.
* **HP & Food:** Influences survival duration and how effectively you can heal.

### Recommended Stat Targets by Level Range

#### Levels 1–5: Fire Ant Phase (Prioritize Precision & Crit)
Damage output is naturally low due to a lack of skill points. Focus completely on consistency.
* **Precision:** ~65%
* **Crit Chance:** 20–25%
* **Attack:** 140–160
* **Crit Damage:** 140–160
* **Armor / Dodge:** 8–12 *(not an early priority)*
* **HP:** 60–80
* **Hunger Limit:** 4–5
* **Core Focus Order:** `Precision` $
ightarrow$ `Crit` $
ightarrow$ `Attack`
* *Reason:* Missing shots penalizes low-level damage profiles heavier than any other stat omission.

#### Levels 7–10: Scorpion Phase
Skill points begin flowing enough to survive basic interactions without instantly dying.
* **Core Focus Order:** `Precision` $
ightarrow$ `Armor/Dodge` $
ightarrow$ `Crit`
* *Strategy:* Begin building up Armor and Dodge to extend survival while maintaining high accuracy benchmarks.

#### Levels 12–16: The Crit Phase
Accuracy is stable, and critical strike profiles spike overall damage numbers.
* **Core Focus Order:** `Crit` $
ightarrow$ `Crit Damage` $
ightarrow$ `Attack`
* *Strategy:* Intensely boost critical chance/damage variables while maintaining a healthy baseline Precision of 65–70%.

#### Levels 18–30: High-Damage Era
Transitioning into full localized combat builds.
* **Core Focus Order:** `Damage` $
ightarrow$ `Crit` $
ightarrow$ `Everything`
* *Strategy:* Heavily increase Attack scaling, sustain strong Crit and Precision pools, and shore up Armor/Dodge limits.

### Level 20+ Ideal Stat Baseline
By level 20, players should strive to balance massive damage output alongside durable survival baselines:
* **Attack:** 180–200
* **Precision:** 70–75%
* **Crit Chance:** 35%
* **Crit Damage:** ~160
* **Armor:** 12–16
* **Dodge:** 12–16
* **HP:** 70–80
* **Hunger:** 5–6

### Summary Philosophy
* **Very Low Level:** Damage is minimal, hits matter most $
ightarrow$ **Maximize Precision**.
* **Low–Mid Level:** Balanced medium damage profiles $
ightarrow$ **Build Precision + Basic Survivability**.
* **Mid–High Level:** High burst potentials $
ightarrow$ **Maximize Crit + Crit Damage**.
* **High Level:** All-around structural strengths $
ightarrow$ **Boost everything, specialized toward Attack + Crit**.
